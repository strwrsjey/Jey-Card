FOR JEY

"Is it better to speak or to die?" A question i ask myself everyday for i used to believe silence was safety. That if i mashed all the poems and tears that lie within me i would be loved softly. That what lived in my soul was not meant to be unmasked for what if no one listens. But i choose to speak even if it bills the girl i used to be. I choose to speak for the girl who wrote letters no one heard. For it is a voice that matters because who am i without the words that make me who i am.
